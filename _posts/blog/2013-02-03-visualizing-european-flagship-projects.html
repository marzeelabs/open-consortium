In order to understand collaborative science projects, a geographical visualization of the research network is often helpful. We have mapped out the partners of the Human Brain Project, one of the European FET projects that will address one of the greatest challenges of modern science: understanding the human brain.

The Human Brain Project federates more than 80 European and international research institutions, and is planned to last ten years (2013-2023). Use the interactive map below to discover the partners involved in this massive European science network.
