Tailor made dissemination strategies for your results

A professional science communication strategy boosts the impact of your work. It helps you secure funding, attracts talent, enhances your reputation and raises awareness of your science. We help you defining your communication goals and target audiences. We assist in finding the right message and the right media to effectively communicate what you have to say, how, where and when you want to say it.

  • We design your communication strategy

    We develop an individually tailored and professional communication strategy for you. We help you defining your communication objectives and setting realistic goals. We assist in identifying relevant target audiences and in choosing the appropriate media and means to reach them. We keep track of and evaluate your impact.

  • We communicate effectively

    We are specialized in cost-effective communication using online media. To communicate tailored to your needs, we work together with your local communication department as well as with a partner network, covering video, illustration and design, data visualization, the organization of science events, communication workshops, press releases and more.

  • We take care of all formal requirements

    We make sure you comply with your reporting obligations both during and at the end of the project. Ideally, we already support you during the project application with bespoke information on communication, dissemination and impact assessment, thereby increasing your chances of funding success.

  • We know how science works

    We are communication and technology experts with science PhDs. We have substantial experience in biomedical research, including several years of work on collaborative EU projects. We understand what you are doing, how and why. Our background helps us getting your message straight, without misinterpretation of your data or oversimplification of your results.
