Communication - your content matters

We are on your side to communicate your science. We report accurately, timely and effectively - always in a form understandable to your target audience. We use online and offline media, text, audio and video. The protection of your unpublished data is a core competence of Open Consortium.

  • We understand what you do

    At Open Consortium, scientists with ample research experience are responsible for communicating your results. No misinterpretation of your data and no oversimplification of your research.

  • We know how to communicate

    We have a five-year track record in communicating science to the general public. We are very experienced in communicating scientific results to groups with specialized thematic focus. Background stories, news updates, interviews and live reporting is all part of the Open Consortium portfolio.

  • You reach your audience

    Open Consortium designs and implements sustainable social media strategies to specifically reach audiences that care. We maximise your impact by using different communication channels and media - all according to your needs. We also write press releases.

  • Your data is safe

    We use an established strategy to ensure that only information meant for the public domain gets out there. We know the meaning of unpublished data and of embargoes and we respect what is meant to be kept internal or private.
