Open job application in Sales

Barcelona-based startup Open Consortium is looking to build up its sales team. We are looking for someone to generate sales leads for our prospective clients, European-funded research consortia. Your job will be to perform online research about European projects, make email and phone campaigns, and maintain our in-house database. Remote working is possible, or on location from Marzee Labs, our beach-based co-working office.

Requirements
  • A professional and independent, pro-active work attitude
  • Proficient in English, both spoken and written
  • Interest to learn about sales in an independent manner
  • Interest in client-focused work
  • Bonus: experience with European research projects, either as researcher or manager
  • Bonus: experience with open-source content-management systems (e.g. Drupal, WordPress)
Benefits
  • Commission-based work; for every successfull sales lead you will receive a percentage of up to 10%.
  • A flat fee for each batch of sales leads, negotiable
  • Free office space to work on your other projects
  • Decide your own hours: from half-time to full-time

If you are a proactive person with a strong customer service focus, take pride and ownership in your contribution, have excellent attention to detail and want to be part of a growing organisation this could your opportunity.

Please email your application to peter@openconsortium.eu

About us
  • Young team of ex-scientists with an interest in European research and web technology
  • Our clients include the EMBL (Heidelberg, Germany), CRG (Barcelona, Spain), Hospital Clinic (Barcelona, Spain) and others
  • Open Consortium is a project of Marzee Labs, an internationally recognized Drupal agency with a team based in Spain and Portugal and working for clients in Spain, Germany, UK, USA.
