Technology to power your Consortium

We speak the language of science and take your headache away with our technology solutions. Our modular packages include web solutions for external communication based on open-source software, design of the identity for your consortium, as well as data and knowledge sharing infrastructure and full laboratory information management systems for handling internal data exchange.

  • Highlight researchers and their research

    Every researcher has a profile that they can easily update. No need to ask everyone to send you Word documents with biography, current research topics or selected publications. And you can announce your jobs on the website so you can hire talented researchers for your Consortium. Website Package

  • Visualize your Consortium

    There is no better way to understand your network than seeing it on a map. We automatically put your researchers on an interactive map. Website Package

  • It looks great... everywhere!

    We follow a systematic approach in designing your identity and website, involving you at every step of the process. And we guarantee that your website will look splendid on every device, whether it is your smartphone on the road, your tablet in the sofa or your computer screen at work. Design Package

  • You build your website yourself

    Nobody knows better than you what you want to show on your website, so why don't you build the site yourself? Powered by the popular Drupal content management system, you can add all the content yourself, whether these are pages, menus, researcher profiles, job openings or projects. We can host the site on our servers, or you can host it at your institute. Website Package

Under the hood Built on Drupal 7, the hottest web system in education today (download the fact sheet). Your Open Consortium website can be extended with some over 5000 available modules. Of course, we are there to assist you before, during and after the launch of your Consortium website.
